UNमNĀ’S School-Based Program

We extend our expertise into educational institutions through a comprehensive program for emotional and academic wellbeing, empowering schools to support students, teachers, and parents holistically.

  • Orientation for Teachers: Building awareness and fostering empathy.
  • Student Screening: Using standardized tools to understand student needs.
  • Workshops for Teachers & Parents: Applying preventive and supportive strategies.
  • Group Workshops for Children: Enhancing emotional literacy and social skills.

Our Process for Schools

Our program is a holistic cycle of support, starting with awareness and moving to identification, intervention, and empowerment.

  • 1

    Orientation & Awareness

    We begin by aligning with teachers and parents, building awareness of students’ emotional and behavioural needs to foster empathy and enable early identification.

  • 2

    Screening & Reporting

    Using standardized tools, we conduct student screenings to understand each child’s unique strengths and difficulties, followed by individualized emotional wellbeing reports.

  • 3

    Workshops & Empowerment

    We empower educators, caregivers, and children with practical insights and strategies through targeted workshops to nurture supportive classrooms and homes.
