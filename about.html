About UNमNĀ

Our Mission: Elevating the Mind

UNमNĀ was founded with a mission to transform the one-dimensional view of therapy into a multidimensional healing experience. We are rooted in excellence and compassion, integrating India’s leading mental healthcare principles with global best practices to make therapy more accessible, empathetic, and impactful.

Our founder, Dr. Jyothi S, envisioned a space where individuals, families, and even institutions could find expert, non-judgmental support to navigate life's challenges. We believe that seeking guidance is a sign of strength and a proactive step toward enhancing emotional resilience, gaining clarity, and developing effective life strategies.

At UNमNĀ, we are committed to making mental health care accessible, empathetic, and empowering. Whether you’re an individual seeking personal growth or an institution looking to enhance emotional well-being, we’re here to support you.
